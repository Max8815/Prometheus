Local AI — Private by Design

We deploy and configure local language models that run entirely on your own infrastructure — no data ever leaves your environment. Built for companies where privacy, compliance, or confidentiality make cloud AI non-negotiable.

Own hardware

The most reliable path. We recommend and spec the right machine — a dedicated workstation, server, or even a capable laptop — and handle the full setup. Your model runs locally, always available, zero ongoing API cost.

Private cloud

If on-premise hardware isn't feasible, we can configure a private cloud instance — isolated, access-controlled, and cost-modelled so you know exactly what you're paying before you commit.

Hardware advisory

Not sure what to buy? We advise on GPU specs, RAM, storage, and model selection to match your use case and budget — so you don't over-invest in hardware you don't need or under-spec something that won't perform.
